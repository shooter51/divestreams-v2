# Assign a Boat to a Trip

You can assign a boat to a trip when scheduling it or by editing an existing trip.

## Assigning at Trip Creation

1. Go to **Trips** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Schedule Trip**.
3. Fill in the tour, date, and time fields.
4. In the **Boat** section, select a boat from the **Select Boat** dropdown. The dropdown shows all active boats with their capacity.
5. Complete the rest of the trip form and click **Schedule Trip**.

## Assigning to an Existing Trip

1. Go to **Trips** in the sidebar.
2. Click the trip to open its detail page.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. In the **Boat** section, select a boat from the dropdown.
5. Click **Save**.

## Removing a Boat from a Trip

1. Open the trip edit form.
2. Select the blank option ("No boat") from the Boat dropdown.
3. Click **Save**.

## Boat Shown on Booking Confirmation

When a boat is assigned to a trip, its name appears:
- On the trip detail page in the "Boat" field
- On individual booking detail pages in the Trip Details section
- On the printed booking confirmation (in the "Boat" row)

## Boat Capacity Note

The boat's capacity is shown as informational in the trip scheduling dropdown. It does **not** automatically cap the trip's bookable spots. To limit how many people can book the trip, set the **Capacity** override field when scheduling the trip. See [Trip Capacity](../trips/trip-capacity.md).

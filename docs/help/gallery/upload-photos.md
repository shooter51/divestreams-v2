# Upload Photos

You can upload photos to an album from the album detail page. DiveStreams converts all uploads to WebP for efficient loading on the public site.

## Upload to an Album

1. Go to **Gallery** in the sidebar.
2. Click the album you want to add photos to.
3. On the album detail page, click **Upload Photos**.
4. Select one or more image files (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF, max 10 MB per file).
5. Optionally add metadata for the batch:
   - **Title** — shared title applied to all uploaded photos in this batch
   - **Description** — optional caption
   - **Location** — dive site or place name
   - **Photographer** — photographer credit
   - **Tags** — comma-separated tags (e.g. "reef, macro, turtle")
6. Click **Upload**.

DiveStreams processes each file, generates a thumbnail, and adds the images to the album. You'll see a success message showing how many images were uploaded and any that were skipped (e.g. oversized files or invalid formats).

## Supported File Types

| Format | Accepted |
|--------|---------|
| JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg) | Yes |
| PNG (.png) | Yes |
| WebP (.webp) | Yes |
| GIF (.gif) | Yes (static only) |

All images are converted to WebP on upload.

## File Size Limit

Maximum 10 MB per file. Files larger than 10 MB are skipped and listed in the upload results.

## Editing a Photo

1. On the album detail page, click a photo.
2. The photo detail panel opens. Click **Edit**.
3. Update the title, description, location, photographer, or tags.
4. Click **Save**.

## Reordering Photos

Drag and drop photos in the album grid to change their display order. The order is saved automatically.

## Deleting a Photo

Click a photo in the album grid, then click **Delete** on the photo detail panel. Deleted photos are removed permanently from the album and from storage. This cannot be undone.

## Photos on the Public Site

Uploaded photos appear on the album's public page when:
1. The album is marked **Public**.
2. The **Gallery** page is enabled in **Settings** → **Public Site** → **General** → **Pages**.

# Tank & Gas Selection

Some tours require that tank type and gas mix are specified at the time of booking. This allows your team to prepare the correct tanks for each diver before the trip departs.

## Setting Up Tank Selection on a Tour

To require tank selection for a tour:

1. Go to **Tours** in the sidebar.
2. Open the tour or click **New Tour**.
3. In the **What's Included** section, check **Require tank & gas selection**.
4. Save the tour.

Any trip scheduled from this tour will require tank and gas selection during booking.

## Tank Types

Your shop creates tank types in the equipment section. Each tank type has a name (e.g. "12L Steel", "15L Aluminium") that appears in the booking form. See [Tank Setup](../equipment/tank-setup.md).

## Gas Types Available

When selecting gas for a tank, the following options are available:

- **Air** — standard compressed air (21% O2)
- **Nitrox 32%** — enriched air, 32% oxygen
- **Nitrox 36%** — enriched air, 36% oxygen
- **Trimix** — helium/oxygen/nitrogen blend
- **Oxygen** — pure oxygen (technical diving)

## How Tank Selection Works During Booking

When you create a booking for a trip that requires tank selection:

1. A **Tank & Gas Selection** section appears under Participants.
2. For each participant, you see a panel labelled "Participant 1", "Participant 2", etc.
3. For each participant, either:
   - Select the **tank type**, **gas type**, and **quantity** from the dropdowns, or
   - Check **Bring own tanks** if the diver is supplying their own.
4. You must cover every participant — if any participant is not configured, the form will not submit.

If the tour does **not** require tank selection, the section still appears but is optional. You can leave it blank.

## Optional Tank Selection

If a tour does not require tank selection but your shop offers gas fills or tank rentals, you can still use the tank selection section to record what each diver needs. This is useful for trip planning even when it's not strictly required.

## Trip Manifest

On the trip detail page, the manifest shows each booking with its tank and gas selections. This gives your divemasters a complete overview before departure.

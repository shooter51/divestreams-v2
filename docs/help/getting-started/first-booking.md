# Take Your First Booking

Before you can take a booking you need:

1. At least one **Customer** in your account
2. At least one **Trip** scheduled (which requires an active **Tour**)

## Step 1: Add a Customer (if needed)

1. Click **Customers** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Customer** in the top right.
3. Enter the customer's **First Name**, **Last Name**, and **Email** (all required).
4. Click **Save Customer**. An email is sent to the customer inviting them to set their password.

## Step 2: Create the Booking

1. Click **Bookings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Booking** in the top right.
3. **Select Customer** — choose from the dropdown or click **+ Add New Customer** to create one on the spot.
4. **Select Trip** — choose the scheduled trip from the dropdown. The list shows only upcoming trips with available spots. Each option shows the tour name, date/time, price, and available spots.
5. **Number of Participants** — enter how many divers are on this booking (minimum 1).

### Optional: Participant Name

You can enter the name for Participant 1 in the participant details section.

### Optional: Tank & Gas Selection

If the tour has **Require tank & gas selection** enabled, this section becomes mandatory. For each participant, either:
- Select a **tank type** and **gas type** from the available options, or
- Check **Bring own tanks** if the diver is self-sufficient.

### Optional: Equipment Rental

Check any rental items the customer wants to add to their booking. Available items (those marked rentable and in stock) are shown with their price.

### Optional: Notes

- **Special Requests** — visible to staff; also sent to the customer in their confirmation email.
- **Internal Notes** — visible to staff only; not sent to the customer.

## Step 3: Set the Booking Source

Select where the booking came from: Direct, Website, Partner, Repeat, Referral, or Other. This helps with reporting.

## Step 4: Confirm

Click **Create Booking**. DiveStreams:
- Creates the booking and assigns it a booking number (e.g. `DS-00042`)
- Deducts the participant count from the trip's available spots
- Sends a confirmation email to the customer (if email notifications are enabled in Settings)
- Redirects you to the **Bookings** list

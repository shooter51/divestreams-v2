# Create Your DiveStreams Account

DiveStreams gives your dive shop or dive tour operation its own subdomain and management portal. This article walks you through signing up and getting access to your dashboard for the first time.

## Sign Up

1. Go to [divestreams.com](https://divestreams.com) and click **Get Started** or navigate to the sign-up page.
2. Enter your **Email address** and choose a strong **Password**.
3. Enter your **Shop name** — this becomes your subdomain (e.g. `yourshop.divestreams.com`).
4. Click **Create Account**.

DiveStreams creates your tenant and redirects you to your new dashboard.

## Log In to Your Dashboard

Your dashboard lives at `yoursubdomain.divestreams.com/tenant`. To log in:

1. Navigate to `yoursubdomain.divestreams.com/tenant/login`.
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3. Click **Sign in**.

> **Tip:** Bookmark your dashboard URL so you can access it directly.

## Recover a Forgotten Password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email address on your account.
3. Click **Send reset link** — check your inbox for a password reset email.
4. Click the link in the email and enter a new password.

## What Happens Next

After signing in for the first time, DiveStreams takes you straight to your **Dashboard**. From here you can:

- Add your first tour (go to **Tours** in the sidebar)
- Schedule a trip (go to **Trips** in the sidebar)
- Add customers (go to **Customers** in the sidebar)
- Customize your public site (go to **Settings** → **Public Site**)

See [Dashboard Overview](./dashboard-overview.md) for a tour of the main interface.

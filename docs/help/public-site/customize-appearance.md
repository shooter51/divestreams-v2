# Customize Appearance

You can change the look and feel of your public site — theme, colors, font, and hero image — from the **Appearance** tab in Public Site Settings.

## Open Appearance Settings

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Public Site**.
3. Click the **Appearance** tab.

## Choose a Theme

Select one of five preset themes. Each theme sets a coordinated color palette:

| Theme | Palette | Best For |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Ocean** | Blue tones | General dive shop (default) |
| **Tropical** | Teal/green tones | Warm-water destinations |
| **Minimal** | Grey/neutral | Clean, modern look |
| **Dark** | Dark charcoal | Bold, dramatic sites |
| **Classic** | Navy and gold | Traditional or upscale shops |

Selecting a theme pre-fills the primary and secondary color fields for that palette. You can then override the colors manually.

## Set Custom Colors

- **Primary Color** — used for buttons, links, and key highlights. Enter a 6-digit hex code (e.g. `#0ea5e9`) or use the color picker.
- **Secondary Color** — used for secondary accents and hover states. Enter a hex code.

Colors must be in `#RRGGBB` format. Invalid formats will show an error when you try to save.

## Choose a Font Family

Select the typeface for all text on your public site:

| Font | Character |
|------|-----------|
| **Inter** | Modern, highly legible (default) |
| **Poppins** | Rounded, friendly |
| **Roboto** | Clean, geometric |
| **Open Sans** | Versatile, widely used |

## Set a Hero Image

The **Hero Image URL** field sets the background image for the top section of your home page. Paste the URL of a publicly accessible image (JPEG or WebP recommended). The image is displayed with an overlay to keep text readable.

> **Tip:** Host your hero image in your gallery or on a CDN and paste the URL here. Recommended minimum width: 1920 px.

## Save

Click **Save Appearance Settings**. Changes take effect immediately on your public site.

## Preview Your Site

After saving, click the site link shown at the top of the Public Site page to open your site in a new tab and review how your changes look.

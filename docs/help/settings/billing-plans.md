# Billing & Plans

The **Billing** page shows your current subscription plan, usage, and payment details. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription here.

## View Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Billing**.

The top section shows:
- Your current plan name
- Current billing period and next billing date
- Usage summary (e.g. bookings this month, team members)

## Plans

DiveStreams offers several plans. The exact plans and prices shown depend on what is configured for your account. Typical plans include:

| Plan | Tours/Month | Customers | Team Members | POS | Integrations |
|------|------------|-----------|-------------|-----|-------------|
| Standard | 25 | 50 | 1 | No | No |
| Professional | Unlimited | Unlimited | 10 | Yes | Yes |
| Pro | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Yes | Yes + white-label |

## Upgrade Your Plan

1. On the Billing page, find the plan you want under **Available Plans**.
2. Click **Upgrade** on the plan card.
3. You are redirected to the Stripe checkout page.
4. Enter your card details and complete payment.
5. Your plan upgrades immediately on success.

## Manage Your Subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Stripe billing portal. From there you can:
- Change your payment method
- Switch between monthly and yearly billing
- Download past invoices
- Cancel your subscription

## Payment Method

Your card on file is shown on the billing page (brand and last 4 digits). To update your card, click **Manage Subscription** and update it through the Stripe portal.

## Invoice History

Past invoices are listed on the Billing page. Click the download icon on any invoice to get a PDF.

## Cancelling

To cancel your subscription, click **Manage Subscription** and select **Cancel Plan** in the Stripe portal. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period, then reverts to the free Standard plan limits.

## Plan Limits

When you reach a plan limit (e.g. maximum customers or team members), DiveStreams shows an upgrade prompt. You can still view existing data but cannot create new records until you upgrade.

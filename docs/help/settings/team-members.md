# Team Members

The **Team** page lets you invite staff, assign roles, and manage access. Only the **Owner** and **Admin** roles can manage team members.

## View Your Team

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Team**.

All current team members are listed with their name, email, role, and status. Pending invitations appear in a separate section below.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything — billing, settings, all modules |
| **Admin** | Bookings, customers, trips, tours, equipment, boats, reports; cannot change billing |
| **Staff** | Bookings, customers, trips only |

## Invite a Team Member

> **Requirement:** Inviting team members requires a paid plan. On the Standard (free) plan you cannot invite additional users.

1. On the Team page, click **Invite Team Member**.
2. Enter the person's **Email Address**.
3. Select their **Role** (Admin or Staff).
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

The person receives an email with a link to accept the invitation. Once accepted, they appear in your team list.

> **Note:** If the person already has a DiveStreams account, they are added immediately. If they already have a pending invite, you'll see an error — wait for the original invite to expire before sending a new one.

## Pending Invitations

Pending invites are listed below the team members table. Each shows the invitee's email, role, invite date, and expiry date. You can cancel a pending invitation by clicking **Cancel** next to it.

## Changing a Team Member's Role

Click **Edit** next to the team member, select a new role from the dropdown, and click **Save**. The change takes effect immediately.

## Resetting a Team Member's Password

1. Find the team member in the list.
2. Click **Reset Password**.
3. Enter a new temporary password in the modal and click **Save**.
4. Share the temporary password with the team member. They can change it after logging in via their profile settings.

## Removing a Team Member

Click **Remove** next to the team member. They lose access to your account immediately. Their historical activity (bookings recorded, etc.) is preserved.

## Team Member Limit

Your plan determines how many team members you can have. The current count and limit are shown at the top of the Team page. Upgrade your plan to add more staff.

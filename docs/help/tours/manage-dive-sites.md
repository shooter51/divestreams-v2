# Manage Dive Sites

**Dive Sites** are locations where your tours take place. Once created, you can associate dive sites with tours so that customers see the locations when browsing your public site.

## Add a Dive Site

1. Go to **Dive Sites** in the sidebar (under Tours, or directly from the nav).
2. Click **Add Dive Site** in the top right.
3. Fill in the required fields:

### Basic Info

- **Site Name** (required) — e.g. "Blue Hole Wall" or "Shark Point"
- **Description** — a public-facing description of the site

### Dive Details

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Max Depth (meters) | Yes | Maximum depth in metres (1–1000) |
| Difficulty | Yes | Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Expert |
| Typical Conditions | No | Free text, e.g. "Mild current, 20–25m visibility" |

### GPS Coordinates

Enter **Latitude** and **Longitude** in decimal degrees (e.g. 7.165, 134.271). These are used for navigation display on your public site.

### Highlights

Enter a comma-separated list of key features (e.g. "Manta rays, Coral bommies, Sea turtles"). Each item appears as a tag on your public site.

### Images

Upload up to 5 images (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF; max 10 MB each). You can add more images after creation from the dive site detail page.

### Status

Check **Active** to make the site available for use. Inactive sites will not appear when associating sites with tours.

4. Click **Add Dive Site**. You are redirected to the site detail page.

## Associate Dive Sites with a Tour

Dive sites are linked to tours from the **Tour detail page**:

1. Go to **Tours** in the sidebar.
2. Click the tour name.
3. In the **Dive Sites** section, click **Add Dive Site** and select from your list.

Once a tour has associated dive sites, they appear when scheduling a trip from that tour (shown as a read-only info panel in the **New Trip** form).

## Edit a Dive Site

1. Go to **Dive Sites** in the sidebar.
2. Click the site name.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Make your changes and click **Save**.

## Deactivate a Dive Site

If a dive site is no longer in use, set it to **Inactive** from its edit form. It will stop appearing in tour associations and in the public site.

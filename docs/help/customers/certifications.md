# Customer Certifications

DiveStreams stores diving certifications on customer profiles. This helps you quickly confirm a diver's qualification level before a tour.

## Recording a Certification When Adding a Customer

When you create a new customer, you can record one certification in the **Certification** section:

1. Select the **Agency**: PADI, SSI, NAUI, SDI, BSAC, or Other
2. Select the **Level**: Open Water, Advanced Open Water, Rescue Diver, Divemaster, or Instructor
3. Optionally enter the **Certification Number** (their card number)

## Adding Certifications to an Existing Customer

To add or update certifications for an existing customer:

1. Go to **Customers** in the sidebar.
2. Click the customer's name.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Update the certification fields.
5. Click **Save Customer**.

Currently, DiveStreams stores one certification record per customer on the create form. Additional certifications can be managed from the customer profile.

## Certification Levels Supported

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Open Water | Entry-level certification — suitable for most recreational tours |
| Advanced Open Water | Qualified for deeper dives and specialty experiences |
| Rescue Diver | Can assist in emergency situations |
| Divemaster | Professional-level; can lead certified divers |
| Instructor | Qualified to teach and certify divers |

## Using Certifications for Tour Requirements

When you create a tour, you can set a **Minimum Certification** requirement. DiveStreams displays this information on the tour's public page but does not automatically block bookings based on certification level — your staff must verify the customer's certification before the dive.

## Certification Agencies

The following agencies are recognised in the system:
- **PADI** — Professional Association of Diving Instructors
- **SSI** — Scuba Schools International
- **NAUI** — National Association of Underwater Instructors
- **SDI** — Scuba Diving International
- **BSAC** — British Sub-Aqua Club
- **Other** — for any other agency

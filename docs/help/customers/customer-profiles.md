# Customer Profiles

Each customer has a profile page that shows all of their information and booking history.

## Opening a Profile

1. Go to **Customers** in the sidebar.
2. Use the search bar to find the customer by name or email.
3. Click the customer's name to open their profile.

## What's on the Profile Page

### Header

The header shows the customer's name, email, and phone number along with two action buttons:
- **Edit** — opens the edit form
- **New Booking** — opens the new booking form with this customer pre-selected

### Booking History

A list of all bookings associated with this customer, showing:
- Booking number (clickable link to the booking detail)
- Trip name and date
- Number of participants
- Total amount
- Payment status
- Booking status badge

### Certifications

All recorded certifications, each showing:
- Agency (PADI, SSI, etc.)
- Level (Open Water, Advanced, etc.)
- Certification number (if entered)

### Emergency Contact

Name, phone, and relationship of the emergency contact.

### Medical Information

Conditions and medications if any were entered.

### Notes

Internal staff notes.

## Editing a Customer Profile

1. Open the customer profile.
2. Click **Edit** in the top right.
3. Update any fields.
4. Click **Save Customer**.

You can update any field including name, email, phone, certification details, emergency contact, medical info, and address.

## Deleting a Customer

Customer deletion is not available directly from the dashboard to protect booking history. To remove a customer you should contact support or manage the data via the database directly.

## Customer Login

Your customers can log in to your public site at `yourshop.divestreams.com/login`. Once logged in, they can:
- View their upcoming bookings
- View their booking history
- Update their profile

Customers set their own password via the invitation email sent when you create their account. They can reset it via the Forgot Password flow.
